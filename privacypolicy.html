Privacy & Cookie Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Wezlon Software Development Academy.
We are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and protecting your personal information.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data
when you interact with our website, programs, and services. By using our website and services,
you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal information:

Contact Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number,
and postal address, which we collect when you communicate with us or register for our
courses and services.

Usage Information: We may collect data on how you interact with our website and services,
including IP addresses, device information, browser type, and pages visited. This information helps us
optimize our services and personalize your learning experience.

Account Information: When you create an account with us, we collect and store your username,
password, and any preferences you set to personalize your user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

Providing Services: To deliver the educational programs, products, and services you request, such as processing enrollments and providing customer support.

Improving Services: To analyze usage data, improve our online courses and platform, and develop new educational
tools tailored to your preferences.

Communication: We may use your contact details to send updates, course notifications, promotional offers, newsletters, and important
announcements. You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications at any time.

Legal Compliance: We may process your data to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to lawful requests from government authorities.

Data Security

Wezlon Software Development Academy takes data security seriously.
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized
access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmitting data
over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted
third parties in the following cases:

Service Providers: We may share your data with service providers who assist us in managing
our website and delivering educational services.

Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information to comply with legal requirements or to
protect our rights, safety, or property, as well as those of our students and the public.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Wezlon Software Development Academy reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes
in our practices or for legal reasons. We recommend that you review this policy periodically for updates.
